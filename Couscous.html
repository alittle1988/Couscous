Cilantro Couscous

First ill start with Fresh Cilantro Lemon Couscous. This recipe pairs well with any type of fish or chicken. Its quick, easy, and simple.

Ingredients

2 Tablespoons of Oilve oil
2 Garlic Cloves, Minced
.5 Teaspoon Turmeric
1 2/3 Cups of water
1 Teaspoon Finely Grated Lemon Peel
.5 Teaspoon Coarse Kosher Salt
1.5 Cups of Plain Couscous
.25 Cups Chopped Fresh Cilantro
2 Tablespooons Fresh Lemon Juice

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in medium saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and turmeric and stir for about 1 minute.
  3. Add 1 2/3 cups water, lemon peel, and 1/2 teaspoon coarse salt
  4. bring to boil.
  5. Remove pan from heat and immediatly stir in Couscous and cover
  6. let stand until liquid is absorbed for about 5 minutes.
  7. Fluff with fork
  8. mix in cilantro and lemon juice.
  9. Season with pepper and additional coarse salt to taste





Greek Couscous

The second Couscous i will be breaking down is a Greek Couscous. What makes it Greek may you ask, well let me break that down for you. The items that make this dish greek is the Tomatoes, Kalamata Olives, and Fetta Cheese. Very similar to a Greek salad but hold the lettuce and add the food so nice they name it twice Couscous! now lets list some ingredients.


Ingredients

  • .25 Cup Chicken broth
  • .5 Cup Water
  • 1 teaspoon Minced Garlic
  • .5 Cup Pearl (Israeli) Couscous
  • .25 Cup Chopped ?aun-dried Tomatoes
  • .25 Cup Sliced Kalamata Olives
  • 2 Tablespoons Crubled Feta Chesse
  • 1 Cup Canned Garbanzo Beans, Rinsed and Drained
  • 1 Teaspoon Dried Oregano
  • .5 Teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Tablesppon White Wine Vinegar
  • 1.5 Teaspoons Lemon Juice

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Pour the chicken broth and water into a saucepan
  2. Stir in the garlic and bring to a boil
  3. Stir in the couscous
  4. Cover the pan and remove from heat
  5. Allow the couscous to stand until all the water has been absorbed, about 5 minutes
  6. fluff with a fork
  7. Allow the couscous to cool to warm temperature
  8. In a large serving bowl, lightly toss the couscous, sun-dried tomatoes, olives, feta cheese, and garbanzo beans
  9. Mix the oregano, black pepper, white wine vinegar, and lemon juice in a small bowl
  10. pour mixture from small bowl over the couscous
  11. Toss again and serve.



Strawberry Avocado Couscous salad with
Lime Vinaigrette

Last but not least and definitly my favorite. Strawberry Avacado Couscous with Lime Vinaigrette. This recipe is busrting with flavor! From the Sweet bits of strawberries to the tart bite of the lime vinaigrette. This recipe is definitly a crowd favorite. From family dinners to social potluck you will never have leftovers. Lets begin with out list of ingredients

Ingredients

Couscous

  • 1 Cup Couscous
  • 1 Avocado (halved, seeded, peeled and diced)
  • .5 Cup Corn Kernels
  • .5 Cup Strawberries (quartered)
  • 2 Tablespoons Chopped Fresh Cilantro Leaves
  • 2 Tablespoons Pine Nuts

Lime Vinaigrette

  • .25 Cup Olive Oil
  • .25 Cup Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Zest of 1 Lime
  • 2 Tablespoons Freshly Squeesed Lime Juice
  • 2 teaspoons of Sugar (flavor to taste)


Cooking Instructions:

Lime Vinaigrette

  1. whisk together olive oil, apple cider vinegar, lime zest and juice, and sugar in a small bowl

Strawberry Avocado Couscous

  1. In a medium saucepan, cook couscous according to package instructions
  2. In a large bowl, combine couscous, avocado, corn, strawberries, cilantro and pine nuts.
  3. Stir in lime vinaigrette.
